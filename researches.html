Material & Metallurgical Engineering
Research Highlights
Departmental Research Works

Research 1:Extraction of Zinc from Sphalerite

A comprehensive study into the extraction of zinc from sphalerite ore using both hydrometallurgical and pyrometallurgical processes. Analysis includes roasting, leaching, and purification stages.

Research 2: Development of Sn-Cu/SiO₂ Composite Nanoparticles Solder Flux

An investigation into lead-free solder joints using tin-copper composite nanoparticles infused with silica sand for enhanced mechanical properties and improved solderability.

Research 3: Failure Analysis of Industrial Steel Components

Metallurgical examination and microstructural analysis of failed steel parts from local industries, focusing on fatigue cracks, thermal degradation, and corrosion behavior.

Research 4: Mechanical Properties of Heat-Treated Aluminium Alloys

Study on the effect of different heat treatments on the hardness, tensile strength, and microstructure of common aluminium alloys used in manufacturing.

Research 5: Corrosion Behaviour of Low Carbon Steel in Acidic Media

Evaluation of corrosion rates of low-carbon steel samples in acidic environments and the effectiveness of green corrosion inhibitors.
